描述

探討原子晶體能帶的起源。這些能帶結構決定了物質如何傳導電流。

翻譯者：
許伯任 (2009)
許伯任 (2010)

學習目標

  • Describe how how band structure results in a lattice of many wells.
  • Describe how to go from the microscopic potential of a single atom to the macroscopic potential of a solid.
